Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Starter & Premium packages?
Premium includes protective lenses (blue-light & UV), while Starter comes with standard lenses. The Premium package also features anti-reflective coating and an extended 2-year warranty.
How do I know which frames suit me?
Use our "Eyewear Match" tool — it recommends frames based on your face shape, lifestyle, and preferences. You can also use our Virtual Try-On feature to see how frames look on you before purchasing.
Do you accept returns?
Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for unworn glasses in original condition. Please review our full return policy for details.
What payment methods are available?
We accept Paystack (cards & digital payments), Flutterwave, and direct bank transfer.
How does Virtual Try-On work?
You can either use your device's camera for a live AR preview or upload a photo to test frames. Our technology will map the glasses onto your face so you can see how they look from different angles.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within major cities, and 5-7 days to other locations. Express shipping is available for an additional fee.
