Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Adeola Johnson after noticing increased digital eye strain among friends and colleagues, Iblane Eyewear began as a simple mission: to create stylish, affordable eyewear that protects eyes from the harmful effects of prolonged screen time.

What started as a small project has grown into a passionate team dedicated to combining fashion with function. We believe everyone deserves access to quality blue-light protection without compromising on style.

Our Mission

Adeola Johnson, Founder & CEO

Adeola has over 10 years of experience in optical retail and eyewear design. After completing her degree in Optometry, she noticed a gap in the market for affordable, stylish blue-light glasses that didn't compromise on quality.

Her vision for Iblane is to create eyewear that seamlessly integrates into modern lifestyles while providing essential protection against digital eye strain. She's passionate about combining Nigerian design aesthetics with global trends.

Sustainability & Materials

At Iblane, we're committed to minimizing our environmental impact while delivering premium products. Our frames are crafted from sustainable materials including bio-acetate and recycled metals.

We use minimal, recyclable packaging and partner with carbon-neutral shipping providers where possible. Our goal is to create eyewear that's not only good for your eyes but also for the planet.
